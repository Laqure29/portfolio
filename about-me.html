Who Am I?

I'm Layba, a graphic designer based in New Delhi with over 2 years of experience in crafting digital and print designs that drive brand identity and engagement.

I specialize in visual branding, content strategy, and social media design, with a portfolio of 500+ graphics and successful campaigns for events like iFestival and annual award ceremonies.

Art is my language — it’s how I educate and inspire.

I’ve led end-to-end content creation—including photography, video editing, and event coverage—delivering cohesive visuals across platforms. Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite, I bring strong project management skills and a keen eye for design trends to every project, ensuring impactful and consistent brand communication.
